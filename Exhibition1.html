We Don’t End at Our Edges

March 8, 2025 – June 15, 2025

"We Don’t End at Our Edges" is a bold and immersive exhibition that invites viewers to explore the boundaries between form, identity, and emotion. Showcasing works from emerging and established contemporary artists, this exhibition dives into the abstract language of color, shape, and space.

The featured pieces challenge traditional perspectives and encourage the audience to consider how art blurs the edges between self and surroundings, physical and emotional realms.

Join us for an inspiring journey through thought-provoking installations, dynamic canvases, and multimedia experiences.

Featured Artists

  • Ritika Sen
  • Arjun Mehra
  • Anjali Menon
