Echoes of Silence

October 5, 2025 – December 30, 2025

"Echoes of Silence" is a meditative collection of artworks that reflect on solitude, stillness, and inner dialogue. Through subtle hues, empty spaces, and minimalist aesthetics, this exhibition creates a quiet yet powerful experience.

The collection invites viewers to pause and consider the beauty found in silence and the unspoken.

Featured Artists

  • Devika Balan
  • Rohit Anand
  • Sana Iqbal
