Beyond the Frame

July 1, 2025 – September 20, 2025

"Beyond the Frame" explores how visual boundaries can be transcended through innovation and disruption. This exhibition emphasizes experimental techniques and invites the audience to see what lies outside the traditional canvas.

The works engage with architecture, digital media, and performance, challenging the viewer to break free of formal expectations and redefine their perspective.

Featured Artists

  • Meera Sinha
  • Kabir Rao
  • Leela Nair
